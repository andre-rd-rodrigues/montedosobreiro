About us

Monte do Sobreiro is a classic Alentejo house, whitewashed with its blue bar. Inside, its ceilings are vaulted, typical style of this region, which provides not only a cozy atmosphere but also increases the authenticity of the experience. The house is protected by porches that provide shade as well as invite you to relax outside to the sound of birds chirping. The outside of the house is equally inviting.

On one side there is the swimming pool, with space for sun loungers and parasols, where you can see Arraiolos. On the other side, besides the fruit trees, there is a cork oak tree ideal for barbecues with family and friends, which is promised to feed not only the body but also the soul. The house was designed as a vacation and weekend retreat, providing an atmosphere of tranquillity and charm in a relaxed rural setting.
